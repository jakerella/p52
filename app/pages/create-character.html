Character Quirk

Quirks allow you a lot of freedom: develop a short backstory for your character - where did they come from? Why are they here? Then think of some quirk that they have, maybe they are terrified of the dark, or have a peg leg. Perhaps they were raised by wolves. You can go completely wild here.

There are some example quirks and effects below, but you should feel free to create your own. Be creative and think about how your character would really behave! Some quirks may be ongoing (like a defensive bonus in certain scnearios), or they may be once-per-quest use. For example, having a magic bag that you can pull an item out of just in time.

Note that any quirk must have both a bonus AND a penalty wihtin the game mechanics!
