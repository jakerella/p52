    Pick Up An Item

    You may pick up any item on your current spot or traded to you by another player on this spot. You might also aquire items from enemy drops, trades with other players, or quest bonuses! This is the place to add those items to your inventory.

    Open a Chest

    You can open a chest in three different ways. You will need to update your game board (and possibly character sheet) depending on the outcome!

    Lock Picking

    You are not trained in lock picking, and do not have a lock pick!

    You may open a chest using the Lock Picking ability and/or one or more lock pick items.

    If a player is not trained in the Lock Picking ability, they may use a Lock Pick item to attempt to open the chest. For this method, each lock pick item you use allows you to assume a higher level in the Lock Pick ability for this turn. In other words, using a single pick is like having level 1 Lock Picking. Using two pick items is like having level 2 in the ability (1 + 1). You cannot get above level 3 using this method.

    If you are already trained in the Lock Pick ability, using a lock pick item reduces your ability target by -1 (again, a maximum of 3 picks may be used).

    A failed attempt with a lock pick destroys the lock pick. (The app will remove the correct number from your inventory.)

    Attacking the Chest

    If the lock on a chest has been broken - or if you simply choose not to use the Lock Picking ability or a Lock Pick item - you may attack the chest in order to open it. This will primarily use your Think ability.

    Warning: there is a chance you might destroy the chest and any item in it! You might also cause yourself damage. However, you may use Stamina to increase your flip value.

    Using a Chest Key

    You do not have a chest key!

    A chest key will automatically open a chest successfully. Using this item consumes your turn and consumes the key! (The app will automatically remove the item from your inventory after you open the chest.)
